About

I am Chanhui Lee, a researcher interested in generative models and visual representation learning. My current work explores how latent spaces, autoencoding models, and diffusion pipelines can be transferred, adapted, and understood across visual domains.

I like building research code that is clear enough to inspect and robust enough to iterate on. This page collects research updates, selected publications, and notes as they become public.
